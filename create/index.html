• Choose on the sliders the number of rows and columns.
  • Press 'I' and click on a square to place the starting position.
  • Press 'F' and click on a square to place the end position.
  • Press 'W' and click on a square to place a wall - Backspace to remove the last.
  • Press 'P' and drag through squares to determine the route of a given police. Backspace to remove the last.
